Ethan Long - Photo Essay

American Individualism presents opportunities to the populace to follow their desires to achieve life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness while maintaining order and equality through the social contract and common good that is unique in America. From rags to riches, self-made individuals constantly send waves across society that positively impact others to achieve all they can. The unique journey that everyone undertakes to achieve their goals and dreams while coexisting with everyone who is also on their own path turns American society into heartfelt stories that inspire others across the world to the betterment of their own lives.

The Full Circle

Your American dream is affected by someone else, ranging from your parents to your friends. You start to dream and create ambitions for your future. These wishes constantly change depending on your mood, impressions, or current interests. While this is your beginning, it is also the sunset for someone else reaching the full circle and achieving all they can. From past generations to future generations, the American Dream is passed on through you.

The Big Picture

The American Dream is a cycle that exists within the United States and passes generation to generation with fluctuation and reformation to contend to new the new desires and dreams of the young. It is a general concept that all Americans can follow their heart's desire as long as it follows the social contract that benefits the general population. The want for security, peace, wealth, education, family all matter differently to each and every person but in the end, we all want to be able to pursue the concepts and pass the ability among our future generations.
